About the Conference

Navigating Innovations in Education

The CRSM National Educators (Virtual) Conference 2025 is a landmark event aimed at equipping Nigerian educators with the skills, insights, and collaborative tools necessary to thrive in a rapidly evolving educational landscape. With a focus on innovation, inclusivity, and future-readiness, this conference serves as a platform for transformation and nationwide impact.

  • Embrace innovative teaching practices for modern classrooms
  • Develop skills to prepare students for the future
  • Collaborate with educators, policymakers, and innovators across Nigeria

CONFERENCE AGENDA

What to Expect at the Conference

Welcome Address

“Embracing Change: Educational Innovations for Nigeria’s Future” – A warm introduction to the goals of the conference and the need for innovation in education.

Keynote Address

“Innovative Trajectories: Crafting the Future of Education” – An inspiring session on revolutionary approaches and possibilities in Nigerian education.

Symposium 1

“Bridging the Digital Divide” – Explore strategies for ensuring equitable access to technology and reducing disparities in digital education.

Symposium 2

“Future-Ready Skills” – Discuss essential skills for tomorrow's world and how educators can adapt their teaching to build these capabilities.

Panel Discussion

“Sharing Success Stories” – Hear real stories of innovation and transformation from educators and stakeholders across Nigeria.

Q&A Session

An interactive forum for attendees to ask questions, share insights, and explore actionable outcomes from the conference topics.
